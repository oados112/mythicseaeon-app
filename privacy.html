Privacy Policy - Mythic Season

Last Updated: January 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mythic Season. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address - For account creation and authentication
  • Username - For display purposes in leaderboards

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Progress - Statistics, levels, points, and achievements
  • Device ID - For displaying ads (Google AdMob)
  • Usage Data - App interactions to improve user experience

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing app functionality and features
  • Saving your game progress
  • Displaying relevant advertisements
  • Improving user experience
  • Providing technical support

4. Information Sharing

We share information with the following third parties:

  • Google AdMob - Ad network (Device ID for targeted advertising)
  • Supabase - Database service (Email, game data for cloud storage)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with service providers necessary for app functionality.

5. Data Security

All information is transmitted via encrypted HTTPS connections. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Object to certain data uses
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Email us at: oados112@gmail.com
  2. Or visit: Delete Account Page

We will permanently delete all your data within 7 business days of your request.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
